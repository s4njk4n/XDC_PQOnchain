How to Test Properly

Because of MetaMask restrictions, you cannot easily test sending to yourself. Follow these steps for successful testing:

  1. Use two different MetaMask wallets (or two different browser profiles).
  2. Send the encrypted message from Wallet A to an address in Wallet B (or vice versa).
  3. Use a small amount of XDC (e.g. 0.01) when testing.

Tip: You can also ask a friend to test with you, or use a second device/browser.
